Why am i writing this?

  It’s April 21,2018, i am in the bad situation of my life. I don’t have a job, failed to pass the post-graduated exam, don’t have a girlfriend, kind of lost myself in daily life. I think it’s time to calm down and have a deep thinking of myself. Ask myself some questions. What i want? What i want to be? Where is my future?
  Time have passed so quickly that i don’t even notice that i am already 23 years old. The experience of my childhood; teenage; my time in the college often emerges in my head.The age 23 isn’t just a number. It means i am 5 years older than a teenager, it means i am an adult. What does the “adult” mean? It means i have to take care of myself. I must have the ability to feed myself. Fullfill my dream if possible. Being an adult doesn’t like being an teenager, it means taking responsibilities. After a few years, my parents gonna retire. The responsibilities of taking care of them and taking care of my family will fall on me. And i have to get prepared for that. If i find a girlfriend oneday, i also have to take care of her. Take the responsibilities of our future.
  Obviously, i am not ready.
  I have a best friend who i really admire. He is facing the reality of life. He sacrifices most of his leasure time to study and work. He has a girlfriend and he need to buy a house for their future. It is the bravory of facing reality that makes me admire him.
  So, i need to make a short term plan for my life.

About health

  What i think most important is health. No matter what job i do, what age i am, health is always the most important thing.
About this i think i am doing quiet well. I do fitness almost everyday. Take a healthy diet. No smoking or drinking. I believe i have to keep this habit. Maybe when i have a job, i won’t have enough time to do this every. But i think i should at least do it 3 days per week.

About job

  After i failed in the post-graduated exam, i have already spent nearly a month in finding a job. I sent lots of my resume, took serval interviews, but still not making ant progress. I should rethinking about the strategy in finding a job. I have been thinking what i wanna do in the future. And it is definitily a program developer. Not because developer earns more than other job, i am really interested in it. I like computer, i’ve been reading computer magazines since i was 13 years old. I think those programmers are super cool, and when i am writing code on a computer, i am quite happy about it. Yes, i know the work of a developer is quite heavy. They usually don’t have much leasure time, i just want to have a try, have a experience of being a developer.
  So, if i want to be a developer in the future, then i should start learning it on my own. In the age of 23, i can’t spend all my time learning it, i need to get a job and learn it in my leasure time. Most of the companies that i sent my resume are big companies; foreign companies. As a person who has already graduated and has no work experience, it’s very difficult for me to get into these companies. All i need right now is just a job with regular rest time so i can use these time to learn Java. Thanks to one of my best friend’s opinion, i am starting to find jobs which is near my home. It don’t have to be big company, short home-work distance and regluar rest time is all i am asking. Good news is i have receiving lots of interview. Hope i will find myself a good one.

About interest

  I have a lot of interests. I want to learn a musicual instrement, and i bought a ukulele. I want to learn japanese, i even sign up for the JLPT. I want to learn other sports like sking and skating. But those things really take time and time is the exactly thing i don’t have. So i think i have to make my time count. Spend less time in playing games, give some time for my interest, even half an hour a day is better than nothing.

About mind & personality

  My mind is becoming a little negative these days, i am receiving to much negative infomations, from the job; from my exam; from my parents. I should just clear my head, focus on what i should do, follow my heart and be positive. But i also shouldn’t be too positive. I should prepare for that if i fail, what i gonna do.

About friends

  I am very happy that i have some best friend who i can talk to. I know them, they know me, i can talk everything with them. And sometimes i really really need to talk with them to share my thoughts or ask for some help mentally.

About financing

  I should start to do some finance management, i used to invest in the stock market. However i lost quite a lot money in it, and i find out it is too risky for me who has little knowledge and experience in it. Maybe i could invest some found or buy some bond. No matter how i manage my money, i really should learn some knowledge first.
